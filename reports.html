Capstone Project

As part of my capstone project for the Portland State University Business School, I lead a team to create a financial model and marketing plan for a restaurant/bar concept. Our team conducted extensive research on the demographic and location factors that impact the success of a restaurant, and we developed a three-tiered marketing plan to reach potential customers. Using financial modeling, we also analyzed three different scenarios for the restaurant/bar concept, including variations in revenue growth and staff requirements. Our analysis allowed us to determine the most profitable scenario and make strategic recommendations for the business.
